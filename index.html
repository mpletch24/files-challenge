Violet Baudelaire

Meet Violet Baudelaire

Violet Baudelaire is the oldest of the three Baudelaire children in A Series of Unfortunate Events. Violet helps her younger siblings, Klaus and her baby sister Sunny solve problems with her inventing skills. You may commonly find her with her hair tied in a ribon to keep it out of her face while concentrating on new inventions. Some of her inventions include:

  • A grappling hook that gets her up Count Olaf's tower in The Bad Beginning
  • A lock pick that enables her to open up Count Olaf's suitcase in The Reptile Room
  • A rubber band ladder to get out of the burning Heimlich Hospital in The Hostile Hospital
  • A fork-assisted climbing shoes that help her and Quigley Quagmire get up the frozen waterfall of Mount Fraught in The Slippery Slope and many more

A Series of Unfortunate Events.

A Series of Unfortunate Events was written by Lemony Snicket and includes thirteen novels that follow protagonists Violet, Klaus and Sunny Baudelaire after their parents' death in an arsonous house fire. After the traggic event, the children are placed in the custody of their villainous cousin Count Olaf who is scheming to steal their inheritance. The children are eventually removed from his care but that doesn't stop their evil distant relative from hunting the children down, bringing other characters down in the process.
